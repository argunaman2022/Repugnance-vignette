Instructions


Please read these instructions carefully. Your understanding of these will be checked in the next page.

In this study, you will read descriptions of various situations. In each situation, a buyer and a seller are planning on engaging in an economic transaction such as selling a specific good or a service. These transactions may not be taking place in your country but they do take place in other countries.

Your task
This study has 2 parts.

Part I
Here, you will evaluate the situations based on various dimensions according to your own opinion. For instance, whether you think this transaction should be allowed or not. In this part, there is no right or wrong answer.

Part II
In Part II, there are right and wrong answers for each situation. Detailed instructions will be given before you begin Part II.

Your payment
Your payment consists of two parts: A completion fee of XXX$ and a bonus payment of up to XXX$.
Your bonus payment depends only on your performance in Part II. One of the questions from this part will be picked randomly to determine your bonus payment.
To maximize your bonus payment carefully read each situation in Part I, as the same situations will appear in both parts.
